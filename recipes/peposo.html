Peposo


Description

An easy-to-make black pepper stew from Tuscany.

Total cooking time: approx. 3 hours

Serves 4


Ingredients

  • 3-4 lbs beef, preferrably cuts that work for pot roasts
  • 4-5 cloves of garlic
  • 3 tbsp of tomato paste
  • 3 tbsp black peppercorns
  • 1/2 tsp milled black pepper
  • 325 ml of red wine, preferrably Chianti
  • 325 ml of beef or vegetable broth
  • Sprig of fresh thyme
  • 2 bay leaves
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Cut the beef into fairly large cubes and set aside in a mixing bowl.
  2. Grate garlic cloves with a grater / microplane, and mix with tomato paste to form a combined paste.
  3. Mix the paste with the chunks of beef to coat al pieces evenly wile adding in salt for seasoning
  4. Crush while black peppercorns roughly using a heave pan or mortar and pestle, then mix with the beef to coat all pieces evenly
  5. Place the beef in cast iron pot, other cooking device appropriate for stewing, dispersing the pieces evenly witin.
  6. Add the wine and broth into the pot, enough to almost fully submerge all the beef.
  7. Add bay leaves and thyme to the cooking liquid, amidst the beef.
  8. Place the pot on the stove and bring to a boil, then lower to a gentle simmer and cover witha heavy lid.
  9. Let the stew cook for 2 - 3 hours, until the beef is fork tender.
  10. Use tongs or other kitchen implement to remove the beef pieces from the pot into another vessel, leaving the cooking in the pot.
  11. Turn stove temperature to high and reduce the wine and broth to the desired consistency.
  12. Cut the heat and return the beef to the pot, mixing with the reduced sauce.
  13. Serve with polenta or bread.
